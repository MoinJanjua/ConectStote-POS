## Description  

Welcome to **ConectStote POS**, your one-stop solution for managing your store's sales, inventory, and customer relationships with ease.  

At **ConectStote POS**, we are dedicated to simplifying your business operations by providing a powerful and user-friendly point-of-sale system.  

---

## Features  

### 1. Track Sales  
- View daily, weekly, and monthly sales summaries.  
- Stay on top of your business performance with detailed analytics.  

### 2. Manage Inventory  
- Efficiently organize products with detailed records.  
- Prevent stock-outs or overstocking with smart inventory management.  

### 3. Build Customer Relationships  
- Maintain detailed customer information to enhance customer experience and build loyalty.  

### 4. Stay Organized  
- Utilize streamlined features to reduce complexity and save time.  
- Focus on growing your business while the app handles the operational details.
